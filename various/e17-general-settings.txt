Enlightenment E17 Settings


1. Change mouse pointer focus
Menu>Settings>All >Windows>Window Focus
Click Window to Focus

2. Disable Main Menu Click
Menu>Settings>All>Input>Mouse Bindings
	# Mouse Button > Left; Menu > Show Main Menu
Delete

3. Screensaver / Suspend timeout
Menu>Settings>All >Screen>Blanking
90mins
Suspend on blank
Suspend even if AC

4. Compositing
Menu>Settings>Modules>Look>Composite
	Load
Menu>Settings>All>Look>Composite>Advance
	Effects:
		# disable guake fullscreen lag
		Apps:
			Style:none
		Engine:
			OpenGL

5. Panel Settings
Right-click on panel > Settings
OR
Menu>Settings>Shelf>Shelf>Settings
	Position:
		Bottom-Left
	Size:
		32 pixel
		Shrink to Content Width
	AutoHide:
		Hide timeout: 0.2
		Hide duration: 0.05

6. Change Titlebar Double click
Menu>Settings>All>Advanced>SignalBindings

	e.event.titlebar > mouse.down.1.double > maximize


7. Placement Center
Menu>Settings>All>Windows> Windows Display
	New Windows > Place at mouse pointer

8. Hide Desktop Icons
Menu>Settings>All>File>FileManager>Display
	Icons on Desktop
