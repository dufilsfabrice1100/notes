## For Chrome/Chromium Browsers
1. Install RSS Subscription Extension (by Google)

https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/rss-subscription-extensio/nlbjncdgjeocebhnmkbbbdekmmmcbfjd?hl=en-US

2. Add Feedly option to your RSS Extension
http://cloud.feedly.com/#subscription/feed/%s

if that one does not work try
http://www.feedly.com/home#subscription/feed/%s
